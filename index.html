AM

Arctic Monkeys

AM is the fifth studio album by English rock band Arctic Monkeys. It was produced by longtime collaborator James Ford and co-produced by Ross Orton at Sage & Sound Recording in Los Angeles and Rancho De La Luna in Joshua Tree, California, and released on 9 September 2013 through Domino Recording Company.

Breaking The Model

New Medicine

New Medicine (formerly A Verse Unsung) is an American rock band from Minneapolis, Minnesota, formed in 2009. They have released two albums “Race You To The Bottom” (2010) and “Breaking The Model” (2014) They continue releasing music and touring today.

Parade

Groundbreaking

Groundbreaking, commonly abbreviated as GB, is an American alternative music project created by Sean Harper. GB has gone through a multitude of different sounds and styles, though mostly revolving around the genres of electropop, alternative rock and electro-industrial.

Human

Three Days Grace

Human is the fifth studio album by Canadian rock band Three Days Grace. The album was released on March 31, 2015 through RCA Records. This is the group's first album without original lead singer Adam Gontier, and the first with My Darkest Days lead singer and Brad Walst's younger brother Matt Walst.
